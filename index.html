Say hello to Sakura Linux.

A minimal, customizable Arch Linux based Distro, with a few cool features. Despite the name, Sakura Linux is vanilla Arch with GNOME.

What do you get with SakuraOS?

Compatibility

Being a Arch Linux based Distro, our minimum requirements are really minimal, so you'll have no hitches installing Sakura on your 10 year old laptop.

Developed on Arch

Sakura Linux is built around Arch. What that means is you can finally brag about using Sakura as a Arch system.

Pure FOSS Bliss

Sakura Linux's source code is publicly available on GitHub. Oh, did we mention we provide guides on how to compile and customize Sakura to your liking?
